Item Shifter
essentials:item_shifter
essentials:item_shifter
<item>Item Shifters/$ are a simple and cheap item transportation method that have 1 inventory slot, and eject any item out the front. They can eject items into an inventory if available, or into the world if not.
A line of <item>Transport Chutes/$ can be attached to the front to allows ejecting items to an inventory several blocks away (default limit: 16).
Handy for moving items cheaply and quickly, including upwards.
